World Happiness

Happiness is an emotional state characterized by feelings of joy, satisfaction, contentment, and fulfillment. While happiness has many different definitions, it is often described as involving positive emotions and life satisfaction and it is important that all of us stay happy and positive throughout our entire lifetime.

World happiness Score in 2019

This is a geographic map that shows the happiness score for each country. From the map, we can see that Finland is the most happy country in the world and South Sudan is the most unhappy country. Happiness of a country is often influenced by several factors which are country gdp, social support available in the country, country's freedom and life expectancy .

Country GDP per capita for top 15 happiest country

From the line chart, we can see that there is a fairly high GDP per capita across the top 15 happiest countries. When a country's GDP per capita is high, residents have more income to sustain their daily needs and therefore able to live happily without worrying much.

Country's freedom for top 15 happiest country

Freedom is a condition in which people have the opportunity to speak, act and pursue happiness without unnecessary external restrictions. Freedom is important because it leads to enhanced expressions of creativity and original thought, increased productivity, and an overall high quality of life. When a country (such as Finland) priotize all these aspects, residents will then be able to gain fully access to their own personal freedom and live happily.

Social support for top 15 happiest country

Research consistently finds that people who perceive their family and friends as supportive report greater happiness than those who doubt their social network's supportiveness. The link between perceived support and happiness reflects both the trait-like personality of support recipients, as well as social interaction. From the chart, we can see that across all these countries, they have contribute significant efforts in providing social support to it's residents.
